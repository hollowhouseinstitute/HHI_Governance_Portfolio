Explicit Co-Creation Model

This work is co-created between a human author and AI systems operating under explicit role constraints.

Roles are intentionally named:

  • AI: structure, synthesis, and implementation within those bounds

Interpretive authority and definition control remain human-governed to prevent silent scope expansion.

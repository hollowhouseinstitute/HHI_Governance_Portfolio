Governance Portfolio (Inspection Surface)

Purpose

This portfolio demonstrates a governance-first framework for evaluating AI systems across time rather than snapshot performance. Governance is treated as infrastructure, not policy text.

Human–AI Co-Creation Model

AI systems are used as co-creators under explicit constraints. They assist with synthesis and structure but do not hold authority.

AI Job Description

  • Draft and synthesize governance artifacts
  • Maintain language consistency
  • Surface options without selecting outcomes

Explicit Non-Responsibilities

  • No decision authority
  • No scope determination
  • No autonomous execution

Why Framework Order Matters

Controls applied out of order fail silently. Each layer in this system depends on the previous one.

Authority → Language → Boundaries → Stop Power → Audit → Infrastructure → Aftercare
